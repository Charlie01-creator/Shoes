✦ Our Story

We Make
Statements
Not Shoes

Charlie Collection was founded in 2013 by Charlie Osei, a self-taught cobbler from East London who grew up obsessing over sneaker culture. With $800 in savings and a borrowed sewing machine, Charlie began crafting custom sneakers in his bedroom.

Within six months, a photo of his handcrafted "Block 1" design went viral on early Instagram. Orders flooded in from every continent. By 2015, Charlie Collection had its first flagship store in Shoreditch, London.

"A sneaker isn't just footwear. It's the first thing people notice and the last thing they forget. We build shoes that make people stop and stare." — Charlie Osei, Founder

Today, Charlie Collection operates in 42 countries, collaborates with world-class athletes and artists, and remains fiercely independent — never compromising on craft, culture, or creativity.

✦ Our Journey

The Charlie Timeline

2013
The Beginning
Charlie Osei hand-stitches his first pair in East London. The "Block 1" goes viral on Instagram within days of being posted.
2015
First Flagship Store
Charlie Collection opens its iconic Shoreditch, London store. 300 people queue overnight for the grand opening drop.
2017
Global Expansion
Stores open in New York, Paris, and Tokyo. Annual revenue surpasses £10M. Charlie is named "Most Exciting Brand" by Hypebeast.
2020
Digital-First Pivot
During the global pandemic, Charlie Collection launches its AI-powered size recommendation and becomes the #1 sneaker brand on TikTok.
2023
10 Year Anniversary
The "Decade Drop" — 10 limited pairs honoring each year — sells out in 4 minutes globally. 50,000+ customer waitlist.
2025
The Future
Phantom X Pro launches. New sustainable materials lab opens. Charlie Collection enters the sportswear and performance market.
