01 — About Us

The Voice of Africa's
Next Generation

The African Future Forum (AFF) is a youth-led Pan-African movement born in Uganda, dedicated to empowering young Africans to discuss, innovate, collaborate, and solve the continent's most pressing challenges.

We believe that Africa's greatest resource is not its minerals, oil, or land — it is its youth. With over 60% of the population under the age of 25, the continent holds an unprecedented demographic dividend.

Youth-Led
Pan-African
Impact-Driven
2019
Founded in Kampala, Uganda

Our Mission

To unite young Africans in dialogue, innovation, entrepreneurship, policy discussions, education, technology, and leadership development that contributes to Africa's transformation.

Our Vision

An Africa where young people actively shape policy, drive innovation, create opportunities, solve local challenges, and influence global conversations.

Our Values

Leadership · Innovation · Accountability · Unity · Opportunity · Pan-African Collaboration · Sustainable Development
