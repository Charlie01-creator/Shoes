About Us

Building the Africa of Tomorrow, Today

The African Future Forum is a youth-led Pan-African movement that unites young Africans across the continent and diaspora. We believe that the solutions to Africa's most pressing challenges lie in the hands of its youth.

Through dialogue, innovation, entrepreneurship, and collective action, we are creating a platform where young leaders can connect, collaborate, and catalyze transformative change across education, technology, governance, and sustainable development.

Our mission is rooted in the belief that Africa's greatest resource is its people — particularly its young people, who represent over 60% of the continent's population.
