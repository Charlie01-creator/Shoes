About Us

Building Brighter
Tomorrows for
Every Child

Parabolic Children Foundation is a registered non-profit organization in Uganda dedicated to transforming the lives of vulnerable children. We believe every child deserves access to basic necessities, quality education, and a loving environment.

Founded in 2015 in Kampala, we have grown from a small community initiative to a recognized foundation serving over 1,250 children across 48 communities. Our holistic approach addresses education, health, nutrition, and emotional wellbeing.

What We Do

Our Core Services

Comprehensive support systems designed to nurture, protect, and empower every child in our care.

Education Support

Providing school fees, uniforms, books, and tutoring to ensure every child receives quality education from primary through secondary school.

850+ children in school

Healthcare & Nutrition

Regular medical checkups, vaccination programs, and daily nutritious meals to keep children healthy and strong.

3 meals daily provided

Safe Shelter

Operating children's homes and foster care programs that provide safe, loving environments for orphans and vulnerable children.

4 residential homes

Arts & Recreation

Music, dance, sports, and art programs that help children express themselves, build confidence, and discover their talents.

12 activity programs

Family Reunification

Working to reunite children with their families when possible, and providing ongoing support to ensure stability and care.

200+ families reunited

Vocational Training

Skills training in tailoring, carpentry, agriculture, and IT to prepare older children for independent, productive futures.

150 youth trained yearly
